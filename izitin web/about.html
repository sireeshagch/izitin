About izitin

izitin connects people with locally sourced products when they become available. Too often the smaller businesses are overlooked because they offer unique product and food alternatives and the public is unaware of their availability. izitin provides an outlet for the business to list their current stock of products and adjust their inventory as needed. Consumers are alerted to product availability and location, even if mobile.


izitin seeks to enhance the small business experience through public awareness, inviting consumers to eat healthy, support local businesses, and enjoy the uniqueness our local businesses bring to our communities.
